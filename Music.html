All About the Different Types of Music

Music is something that everyone can enjoy. Music has the ability of making someone very happy or very sad, depending on the type of music they listen to.

There is a very large variety of types of music. There is pop, rock, rap, alternative, indie, and country. These are just a few of the many types of music.

Music has also made many people very popular in pop culture. People like Ariana Grande, Panic! at the Disco, Taylor Swift, and Drake have been very popular for a while. People like Billie Eilish, Wallows, and the Jonas Brothers have recently came back or recently blew up.
